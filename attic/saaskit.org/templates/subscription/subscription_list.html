Questions & Answers


How does the free trial work?

After signing up and completing your payment details, you won't be charged anything for 21 days. After 21 days your paid plan will start and your first payment will be made. If you cancel your Project account before the 21 day free trial ends, you will not be charged any amount.

Is this a long-term commitment?

Nope! You pay a monthly fee and are able to cancel at any time or upgrade / downgrade to a plan that suits you better.

What forms of payment do you accept?

Payments are via credit card and are processed by PayPal. PayPal accepts most major credit cards such as Visa, Mastercard, American Express & Discover. We do not offer other forms of payment at this time.

How do I use the Poll app?

Upon login you should already be in the Poll App. This trial has been configured to show you the Poll App upon a successful login. You will see a list of Polls. Click on any one of them to give your vote.

How can I create more Polls?

To create more polls login to your app using the Account holder Login details and you will find a link which will help you to create more Polls for your app

What happens to the results of the Polls in my app if I cancel my account?

Your polls will continue to capture results from your users. Upon cancellation of you account you will not be able to view the results.

How can I customize the Polls App?

You should have knowledge regarding Django templates in order to customize the Polls App. You can have a look at the tutorials provided here to get started.

Where can I get a copy of the original SaaS Kit used with the Polls App?

You can visit Github for the bleeding latest version of SaaS Kit
